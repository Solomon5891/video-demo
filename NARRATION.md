## Voice & Delivery Notes
- Pace: relaxed, confident — leave a beat after each section transition.
- Emphasis (bold = hit harder): *FieldTech*, *5 seconds*, *2-way sync*, *23%*, *4 minutes*, *No credit card required*.
- Never name underlying technologies. The brand is **FieldTech**; the magic is **FieldTech**.
- Default to second person ("you", "your truck", "your crew"). The viewer is the technician.
- End on the CTA, not on the brand — the last words the listener should remember are *"Start getting paid."*
